Pre-Engineered Buildings (PEB)

Pre-Engineered steel buildings are designed and fabricated to client's requirements in accordance with the Universal standards.

A pre-engineered steel building consists of four major groups of components: primary members, secondary members, metal roofing/wall cladding and connection fasteners. These components are designed so they are compatible with each other. The fabrication of these components is carried out in factory under strict quality control as per detailed shop drawings.

These components are transported to site with proper markings and assembled at site as per erection drawings. In many regards, detailing for pre engineered steel buildings can define success or failure for a project, for everything from on-time project delivery to quality of workmanship.

With excellent services for pre engineered steel buildings, a project can be completed with high-quality results, reduced costs and reliable time frames for delivery.
